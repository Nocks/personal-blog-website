About Me

Hello there! I'm Enock Kwesi Addey, and welcome to my website. I'm a Python web developer, based in Accra, Ghana. I have strong interests in Python, Django, JavaScript, and CSS. As an autodidact developer, I never stop learning!

I started learning python three years ago, and though it's not my first programming language, it's become my core language. For quality and structure in my learning of Python, I signed up on Treehouse to explore the possibilities of Python; I also later signed up on Dataquest where I studied data analysis using Python. Now, I'm sold on Python as a tool for solving real world problems. I recently earned a Nanodegree in Programming for Data Science for Enterprise.

I have been fortunate to work on some interesting remote projects such as Tripfluencers and Fireside. I'm currently enrolled in Front End Web Developer Nanodegree program. I'm looking forward to getting a full-stack or front-end job after completion, where I get to apply my aquired knowledge and skills on other interesting projects.
